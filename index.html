About Me

A finance professional who reads numbers like a story.

I am an Accounting & Finance Professional with over 14 years of progressive experience across financial accounting, US taxation and regulations, audits, internal controls, and risk assessment. My expertise spans managing full audit cycles, preparing financial statements, analyzing complex transactions, and evaluating internal controls for accuracy and regulatory compliance. I rely on tools like Advanced Excel, NetSuite, SAP, Oracle, QuickBooks, and dashboard creation to turn raw financial data into decisions leadership can act on. I recently completed my Master's in Accounting and Analytics from the Saunders College of Business at RIT, building on a Bachelor of Commerce from Mangalore University, and I am currently awaiting my CPA license. What sets me apart is the breadth of my experience — from KYC operations at JP Morgan Chase, to internal audit at Malabar Gold & Diamonds, to financial controllership for a $100 million transaction volume business — paired with a consistent track record of reducing costs, improving accuracy, and strengthening compliance wherever I've worked. I bring high integrity, professional skepticism, and a genuine enthusiasm for cross-functional collaboration to every role.

Mission

To bring precision, integrity, and data-driven insight to every financial decision I support, helping organizations strengthen controls, reduce risk, and operate with greater efficiency.

Vision

To grow into a trusted financial leadership role where rigorous analysis and clear communication consistently translate into smarter, more confident business decisions.
